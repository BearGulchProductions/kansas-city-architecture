A City Built in Gold

Kansas City rose to prominence during the Art Deco era of the 1920s and 1930s, leaving behind a skyline rich with geometric ornament, gleaming terra cotta, and monumental ambition. It is also the City of Fountains — home to more than 200 public sculptures that animate its grand boulevard system.
